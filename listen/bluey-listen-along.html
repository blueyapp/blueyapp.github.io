Bluey Listen Along

Listen up, squirts! More audio-only versions of your favourite Bluey episodes are available for families as screen-free entertainment.

Designed to relax, engage and ignite the imaginations of children, Bluey Listen Along is the perfect soundtrack to car trips and the ultimate companion for bedtime.

Mixed by Bluey’s own sound designer, Dan Brumm, twenty more episodes are available. The second drop of the Bluey Listen Along series is comprised of fan favourites from seasons 1-3 including Markets, Wagon Ride, Taxi, Bumpy and the Wise Old Wolfhound, The Sleepover, Teasing, Squash, Daddy Dropoff, Army, Escape, Flat Pack, Grandad, Barky Boats, Burger Shop, Octopus, Easter, Curry Quest, Explorers, Pizza Girls, Faceytalk.

Bluey Listen Along is presented by BBC Studios Distribution Kids & Family and ABC in association with Ludo Studio.
